About Us

We cater to small and large-scale industrial maintenance needs with prompt service and expert solutions. We are pleased to introduce ourselves as maintenance-providers and manufacturers of machined components such as motor, gear, boxnut, any machine shaft, key ways, mould machinery, taper cone, nib, rubber/steel roll machining etc.

With a legacy of nearly 50 years of experience and expertise in all aspects of machine repair, rebuilding & maintenance, we have been troubleshooting and refurbishing industrial machinery, and improving machining processes, tool life and cycle times with short consultations here at Nashik since 1978. Hence, we can handle all types of job work arising from small tool room machines to large scale working machines.

We pride ourselves on quick response time and creative alternatives.

We serve a wide range of industries ranging from electronics, electrical devices, textile, polymer, automobile, plastic, rubber, aeronautical, forging, EDM, mining and many more. Kindly see below for a list of some of our customers.

We have expertise in dealing with variety of raw materials: Stainless Steel, Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Aluminium, Copper, Brass, Plastics, Titanium, Nickel Alloys, Aluminium, Phosphor Bronze, Teflon, Bakelite, Cast Iron, etc.

We serve in the entire district of Nashik and other surrounding regions in northern Maharashtra including Mumbai and Pune.

Our Services

  • Machining of components (shafts, keyways, taper cones, etc.)
  • Tool and machinery repair
  • Custom part fabrication using SS, CI, Copper, Teflon, Copper, Phosphor bronze, Alloy steel, Carbon steel etc.
  • Maintenance for textile, electrical, polymer, mining, paper and forging industries
