About Us

With over 45 years of experience, we specialize in maintenance, repair, and manufacturing of machine components like motors, shafts, gearboxes, taper cones, nibs, mould machinery, and more. We serve industries across Nashik, Mumbai, and Pune.

We cater to small and large-scale industrial maintenance needs with prompt service and expert solutions.

Our Services

  • Machining of components (shafts, keyways, taper cones, etc.)
  • Tool and machinery repair
  • Custom part fabrication using SS, CI, Copper, Teflon, etc.
  • Maintenance for textile, electrical, polymer, mining, and forging industries
